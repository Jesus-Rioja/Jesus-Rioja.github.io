Grimoire: Seeking Magic

Grimoire: Seeking Magic was the final project for my degree and my first experience developing a game. Originally meant for Android, it was a PvP strategy card game with some RPG elements where you could choose between a few races to create a character whose stats would directly affect a match. It also had more than 30 cards designed and programmed to interact with the opponent in a strategic battle, encouraging players to have in mind things like their health, armor, character's race, cards available, pets, weapons, card graveyard and their opponent's deck. All of this to win their opponent by reducing their health to 0. By now the project remains as an educational project but the docs and all the desing pattern I followed can be downloaded here.
In colaboration with Marina Ruiz Miñarro.

U.N.F.A.I.R. ft. Shooter

U.N.F.A.I.R. ft. Shooter is an irreverent FPS with Roguelike elements. The action takes place in APE corp laboratories, where a failed experiment leads to absolute chaos in the facilities, turning it in a changing and deceitful place. U.N.F.A.I.R. ft. Shooter will lead the player through a different level each run. Every of these levels will be proceduraly generated having a base seed for the player to rerun anytime. This system generates keys, enemies, hallways, rooms, power-ups and will combine them to achieve unique experience per run. The game will also count with four characters with unique abilities and weapons, all of them designed through a system that allows me to expand their ability set and all of the power-ups they can get in-game without limits by using Scriptable Object, new C# skills and other game design patterns.
